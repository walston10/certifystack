#### Activity C: Display Technology Comparison

**Goal:** Understand different display technologies through hands-on observation.

**Test 1: Black Level Test**

1. Display a completely black image on your phone (search "black screen" images)
2. Go to a dark room
3. Set brightness to maximum
4. Observe: Is the screen truly black or grayish?
   - [ ] True black (likely OLED/AMOLED)
   - [ ] Grayish/backlight visible (likely LCD)

**Test 2: Viewing Angle Test**

1. Display a white screen at 50% brightness
2. Look at screen straight-on, then at a sharp angle (45°+)
3. Does the color shift or dim significantly?
   - [ ] Minimal change (likely IPS LCD or OLED)
   - [ ] Significant color shift (likely TN or basic LCD)

**Test 3: Outdoor Visibility Test**

1. Take your phone outside on a sunny day
2. Set brightness to maximum
3. Rate readability: [ ] Poor [ ] Acceptable [ ] Good [ ] Excellent
4. Your phone's peak brightness: _____ nits (look up spec)

**Test 4: Touch Responsiveness**

1. Open a drawing app
2. Draw diagonal lines slowly across the screen
3. Are the lines smooth or jagged?
   - [ ] Smooth (good touch sampling rate)
   - [ ] Slightly jagged (normal)
   - [ ] Very jagged (poor touch response)
