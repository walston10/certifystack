# Lab 55: Professionalism and Communication

**Tier:** 1  
**Time:** 25-35 minutes  
**Exam Objectives:** 4.7 - Given a scenario, use proper communication techniques and professionalism

---

## Section 1: Concept Check (5 min)

1. Why is it important to avoid using technical jargon when speaking with end users?

2. What should you do if a customer becomes angry or frustrated during a support call?

3. Why is setting proper expectations important in IT support?

4. What is active listening, and why is it important in troubleshooting?

5. What information should be documented after completing a support ticket?

## Section 3: Reflection (5 min)

**Scenario 1:** You're working on a user's computer and accidentally see a personal document open on their screen. It appears to contain confidential information unrelated to the issue. What do you do?

**Scenario 2:** A manager asks you to install unauthorized software on an employee's computer to "monitor their productivity." What should you do?

**Scenario 3:** You realize you made a mistake during troubleshooting that made the problem worse. The user doesn't know yet. How do you handle this?

**Scenario 4:** A VIP executive calls demanding immediate help while you're in the middle of helping another user. How do you handle the situation?
